What is a Mango Graham Cake?

This Filipino icebox dessert consists of layers of graham crackers, sweetened whipped cream, and ripe mango.


Ingredients:

  • 1 packet All Purprose Cream
  • 1/2 cup Condonsed Milk
  • 24 pieces Graham crackers, crushed
  • Ripe mangoes
  • Vanilla extract (optional)

How Do I Make a Mango Graham Cake?

It's easy! Just follow these steps!

  1. Peel the skin off the mangoes. Cutaway the sides (flesh) from the pit. Lay the mango halves flat-side down on a cutting board. Cut the flesh lengthwise into thin slices. (Image 1&2)

  2. Now add the condensed milk and vanilla extract. On medium-high speed, beat everything until smooth and stiff peaks form. (Image 3 to 6)



  3. Line the bottom of a rectangular or square with a layer of Graham crackers. (Image 7)

  4. Then pour 1 part of the cream mixture at the top of the crackers and spread evenly. Then top with a few mango slices. (Image 8 and 9)

  5. Repeat this process until 3 layers are formed, ending with the cream, crushed graham crackers, and cubed mangos on top. You can do lots of layers if you want! (Image 10)



  6. Cover and chill for 8 hours or overnight to set completely before serving.
